I am Dongkyu Kim, an Associate Professor of Political Science at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. My research focuses on international market integration and its interaction with domestic politics. The topics of my research interests include foreign direct investment, social welfare spending, inter-state conflicts, income inequality, political participation, social protest, social capital, and fisheries governance.
