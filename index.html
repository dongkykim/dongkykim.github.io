Welcome

Welcome to my website. My name is Dr. Dongkyu Kim, Associate Professor and Director of the School of Political Science, Public Affairs, Legal & Security Studies and Center for Survey Research & Policy Analysis at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. I earned my Ph.D. in Political Science from the University of Iowa in 2015, with specializations in International Political Economy, Comparative Political Economy, and Political Methodology. My research examines how domestic policies interact with international dynamics across a range of topics, including foreign direct investment, social protest, conflict, inequality, political participation, social capital, and social trust. Through collaborative survey projects at CSRPA, my work has expanded to include local and state politics as well as broader policy issues.
